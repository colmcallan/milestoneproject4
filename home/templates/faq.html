What is the benefits of No More Bugs?

With No More Bugs, you and other promising coders can come together and help each resolve each others bugs in a free enviroment if you're in no rush for a resolution

  • Free sign up
  • Pay to increase the response rate of your query being resolved
  • Up-vote system to allow other users who are having the same issue push the issue to top for a quicker turnaround time
  • Amazing support from myself and other highly skilled professional's in the full stack field

What is "bug" on the website?

The page titled "bug" is where users (who are registered) can post the bug they are facing and wait for others to respond with options on how to resolve the bug. For free!

What is "Ticket" and do I need to use it?

Are you approaching a project deadline and stuck on an error? unsure of a traceback error? or just simply need a quicker response for your issue? Opt for this service and we promise you that you will make your deadline/have your issue fixed within an exceptionally timely manner

Can I get a refund if I'm not happy with the service?

As it stands, our services are a non-refundable product as it is a service we're providing and the outcome is having your issue resolved
